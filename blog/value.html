Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now.

In my previous role, we encountered a situation where we were working on a project, nearing the deadline. However, I discovered that the project's quality wasn't up to par for our clients. Unfortunately, we were running out of time to rectify the issue. It was a difficult moment for me as I had to decide whether to address the problem and delay the handover, or proceed on time with a project of subpar quality.
After carefully considering the advantages and disadvantages, I chose to communicate the true situation to the stakeholders. I convinced them to grant us a little more time to address the issue, aiming to deliver a project of high quality to our client. Initially, the stakeholders weren't very pleased with this decision, but their sentiment changed after receiving positive feedback from our satisfied client.
Looking back, I remain convinced that it was the right decision. Opting to prioritize long-term gains over short-term profit was a wise move. It demonstrates the value of making sacrifices for the sake of achieving lasting success.
